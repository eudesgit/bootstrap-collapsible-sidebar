Bootstrap Collapsible Sidebar Version 1.0

Requirements

  • Bootstrap 4.1
  • JQuery 3.3
  • Material Icons

How does it work?

A collapsible sidebar with 2 levels of submenus, feature icons, titles and separators

You can have items, titles and separators on menus and submenus. Items can open submenus that roll the menu to a submenu. Items can also open a second level submenu that just expands into second level items.

This menu is not suitable to mobile devices. It's set to disapear on any small screen device.
